## DESIGN NOTES FOR GAMMA AI

### Color Scheme
- Primary: Travel-inspired (ocean blue, sunset orange, or forest green)
- Accent: AI/tech (electric blue or purple)
- Background: Clean white or subtle gradient

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or similar)
- Body: Readable sans-serif (16-18px minimum)
- Numbers: Large, impactful (48-72px for key stats)

### Visual Style
- Modern, minimalist
- Mobile-first screenshots
- Data visualizations: Clean charts (bar, pie, funnel)
- Icons: Consistent style (outline or filled)

### Animation Suggestions (Gamma AI)
- Slide 1: Count-up animation for "23 hours" and "$2.3B"
- Slide 3: Progressive reveal of features (Consumer → Pro → Events)
- Slide 4: Hover effects on use case cards
- Slide 6: Animated funnel flow
- Slide 8: Animated pie chart reveal
- Slide 12: Pulsing CTA button
