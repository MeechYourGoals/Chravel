### 1. "Taste Test" Model
**Try Pro features free before paying.** Unlike competitors that lock features behind paywalls, Chravel lets groups experience Pro capabilities (role-based channels, advanced AI, compliance exports) during a trip, then decide if they want to upgrade. This reduces friction and increases conversion.

### 2. Events Bundled, No Per-Head Fees
**One creator pays; everyone else joins free.** Partiful and Luma charge per attendee. Chravel charges one creator for the entire trip/event, making it accessible for large groups, destination weddings, and fan travel.

### 3. Guest Economics
**Only the creator pays; guests join free.** This model removes barriers to adoptionâ€”one person can upgrade to Pro and invite unlimited guests, making Chravel the natural choice for organizers, tour managers, and event planners.

**Like Slack, but for travel logistics.** Pro users get channel-based communication (e.g., "Logistics," "Media," "Payments") with @mentions, threads, and broadcastsâ€”perfect for complex operations like touring artists, sports teams, and corporate retreats.
