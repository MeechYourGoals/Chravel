# Chravel Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Effective Date:** December 2025

## Introduction

Chravel, Inc. ("Chravel," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- First and last name
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional)
- Password (encrypted)

**Trip and Event Data**
- Trip names, descriptions, and destinations
- Itinerary items and schedules
- Chat messages within trips
- Photos, videos, and files you upload
- Expense and payment information
- Task assignments and poll responses

**Payment Information**
- Payment method identifiers (Venmo username, etc.)
- Expense split amounts
- We do NOT store credit card numbers directly

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Push notification tokens

**Usage Data**
- App feature usage patterns
- Session duration and frequency
- Error logs and crash reports

**Location Information**
- Precise location (when using map features)
- Location is only collected when the app is in active use
- You can disable location access in device settings

### Information from Third Parties

**Authentication Providers**
- Google (if using Google Sign-In): Email, name, profile photo
- Apple (if using Sign in with Apple): Email (may be hidden), name

**Google Maps/Places**
- Place names and addresses you search for or save
- We do not share your searches with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide the Service**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Enable trip planning and collaboration features
   - Facilitate group chat and messaging
   - Process expense splits and payment requests

2. **Improve the Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Debug issues and fix errors
   - Develop new features based on usage data

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Send push notifications about trip updates
   - Send email notifications (if enabled)
   - Respond to support requests
   - Send important service announcements

4. **Ensure Safety and Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### With Other Users
- Trip members can see your profile information
- Messages and content you share in trips are visible to trip members
- Your payment method identifiers are visible when requesting payments

### With Service Providers
We share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our Service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database and authentication | Account data, trip data |
| Google Cloud | Maps and AI services | Location queries, AI prompts |
| Firebase | Push notifications, analytics | Device tokens, usage events |
| Stripe | Subscription payments | Email, subscription status |
| Sentry | Error tracking | Crash reports, device info |

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety.

### Business Transfers
If Chravel is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- **Trip Data:** Retained while trip exists; archived trips retained for 1 year after archiving
- **Chat Messages:** Retained while trip exists
- **Media Files:** Retained while associated with a trip; deleted when trip is deleted
- **Usage Analytics:** Retained for 2 years in anonymized form

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Portability
You can access your data through the app's Settings > Privacy > Download My Data.

### Correction
You can update your profile information at any time in the app.

### Deletion
You can request account deletion via Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will:
- Delete your profile and account data
- Remove you from all trips
- Delete your uploaded media (within 30 days)

### Opt-Out Options
- **Push Notifications:** Manage in device settings or app Settings > Notifications
- **Email Communications:** Unsubscribe link in emails or app Settings > Notifications
- **Location Access:** Disable in device settings (some features may be limited)
- **Analytics:** Contact us at privacy@chravel.app to opt out

### California Residents (CCPA)
California residents have additional rights including:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

We do not sell personal information.

### European Residents (GDPR)
EU residents have additional rights including:
- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object
- Rights related to automated decision-making

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at privacy@chravel.app.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- Access controls and authentication

- Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
